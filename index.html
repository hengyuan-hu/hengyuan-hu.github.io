Hengyuan Hu

About

I am a PhD student at Stanford University advised by Dorsa Sadigh. I am interested in imitation learning and reinforcement learning on real robots, as well as huamn-AI coordination.

Previously, I was a Research Engineer at FAIR working with Jakob Foerster and Noam Brown on reinforcement learning, search and coordination in multi-agent environments.

I was a core member of the team that created Cicero, the first human level AI in the complex strategic negotiation game of Diplomacy. I also created the most human-like agent in Hanabi without any human data.
